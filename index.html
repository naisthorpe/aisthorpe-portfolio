About Me

Hi! You can call me Nick.


I moved to Charlotte, NC in the middle of 2019 from the Outerbanks of North Carolina. I have a background in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) with work experience in the NC local government sector. I have worked for three municipalities now - Currituck County as a Development Technician & Planner, Mecklenburg County as a GIS Technician, and the Town of Indian Trail, NC as a Planner.


I've been interested in computers since I was a kid but haven't actively pursued a career in tech until now. My goal is to be able to combine my GIS and local government experience with programming to expand upon my skills and capabilities. I currently have in-class experience with Python, and I'm looking forward to all the fun stuff I will learn in my full-stack web development bootcamp.
